Getting to our Apartment

Here are step by step instructions on how to get to Naa and Ehimah's apartment.


created and designed by Sarah Naa Norkor

    1. At main gate, dial 323.

    Walking? Dial small gate's intercom.

    Driving? Dial big gate's intercom.

    Our phone will ring and we will open the gate.

    2. Walk/ drive straight right in to the last apartment building on your right.

    The apartment is called Ares Court.

    If you are using a car, park at the visitor's carpark infront of Ares Court.
